ABOUT THE

PRODUCT

Tamper Proof SMS Application ensures SMS is completely secured and encrypted to avoid any tampering in its journey from enterprise to telecom operator and finally to the intended recipient.

The application uses advance technology like Blockchain/DLT, asymmetric encryption, cypher technology & digital & biometric signature so that SMS can be delivered without tampering by anyone.

PRODUCT

FEATURES

  • Encryption

    Two layer Encryption of SMS makes tampering almost impossible.

  • TRACEABILITY

    Application provides complete traceability of SMS journey.

  • CONTENT SECURiTY

    Only relevant details will be decoded at nodes (content will remain encrypted).

  • TAMPER ALERT

    If Tamper attempt is made, alarm/alert will be generated. Enterprise sending SMS can block the flagged IP and message.

  • SEAMLESS INTEGRATION

    Easy to Integrate, Configure and Operate.

  • BLOCKCHAIN INTEGRATED

    Blockchain based application has added advantage in terms of enhanced security and collaboration.

ANALYTICS

The application features which enables enterprises to view the entire process information which is related to analytics on a real time basis.

With the tracking feature, Enterprise will have complete visibility of the overall as well as down to the individual MSISDN level SMS delivery related information and connected nodes in SMS journey.
